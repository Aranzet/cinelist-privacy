Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

CineList does not sell your data, show ads, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

1. Introduction

CineList ("we", "our", or "the app") is a mobile application for tracking movies and TV series. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have.

By using CineList, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you sign in with Google or Apple, we receive your display name and email address. Anonymous users have no personal information collected.

Watchlist & Progress Data

Movies and series you add to your list, watch statuses, episode progress, your ratings and reviews — stored locally on your device (Isar database) and optionally synced to our cloud (Firebase Firestore) for cross-device access.

Community Content

AI-generated recaps you save, reviews you write, ideas and bug reports you submit — stored in Firebase Firestore with your user ID and display name.

App Settings

Theme, language, notification preferences — stored locally on your device via SharedPreferences.

3. Third-Party Services

CineList uses the following third-party services:

Firebase (Google)

Authentication and cloud data storage. Your watchlist and community content are stored in Firebase Firestore. We use anonymous authentication as default — no account required to use the app.


Firebase Privacy Policy →
TMDB (The Movie Database)

We use TMDB API to fetch movie and series information, posters, cast, and episode data. Search queries are sent to TMDB servers. No personal data is shared.


TMDB Privacy Policy →
Anthropic Claude API

When you request an AI recap, the title of the movie or series is sent to Anthropic's API to generate a summary. No personal data is included in these requests.


Anthropic Privacy Policy →
YouTube Data API

We use YouTube API to search for video recaps related to movies and series you view. Search queries (movie/series titles) are sent to YouTube. No personal data is shared.


Google Privacy Policy →

4. How We Use Your Information

  • To sync your watchlist and progress across devices
  • To display community recaps and reviews from other users
  • To send local push notifications about new episodes (only if enabled)
  • To improve the app based on your feedback and bug reports

We do not use your data for:

❌ Advertising ❌ Selling to third parties ❌ Profiling ❌ Analytics tracking

5. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored in two places:

  • On your device — watchlist, episode progress, settings (Isar local database)
  • Firebase Firestore — cloud sync of your watchlist, community content you publish

Firebase Firestore is secured with server-side security rules that ensure each user can only read and write their own data. Community content (recaps, reviews, ideas) is readable by all authenticated users.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time:

  • Go to Profile → Account → Delete Account
  • This permanently removes your watchlist from the cloud, your published recaps, reviews, and any other data linked to your account
  • Local data on your device is deleted when you uninstall the app

7. Children's Privacy

CineList is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us.

8. Push Notifications

CineList may send local notifications about new episodes in your watchlist. These notifications are generated on your device and do not involve sending your data to any server. You can disable notifications at any time in Profile → Notifications or in your device Settings.

9. In-App Purchases

CineList offers optional in-app purchases processed through Apple App Store. We do not collect or store your payment information. All transactions are handled by Apple.
